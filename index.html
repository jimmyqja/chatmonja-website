What it does

Built for streamers who want a warm chat without babysitting a bot.

Automatic greetings

Welcome each viewer once per session with rotating Jamaican-style preset greetings.

Custom !commands

Add commands like !socials, !rules, and the default !list.

Role controls

Choose whether everyone, moderators, or the broadcaster can use each command.

Special users

Create custom greetings for regulars, collaborators, and community favorites.

Local backups

Save and restore your greetings, commands, themes, and ignored-bot list.

No ads or telemetry

This release does not include analytics, advertising, or remote crash reporting.

Privacy first

Your bot settings stay on your computer.

ChatMonJA stores Twitch login tokens, greetings, commands, backups, and settings locally. Authentication tokens are not included in backups. Twitch chat traffic is sent directly to Twitch.
